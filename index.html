The Program

What Really Happened is an 18-session, modular, peer-facilitated psychoeducational series that teaches nervous system science, trauma’s impact on relationships, addiction, shame, crisis response, and long-term integration — all without requiring a clinical license.

5-Part Curriculum Roadmap

1. How Trauma Gets Built In

Childhood wiring, nervous system basics, and survival adaptations.

2. How It Destroys Relationships

Attachment injuries, trauma bonding, and interpersonal patterns.

3. Coping Mechanisms & Breaking Points

Addiction, high-functioning dissociation, and self-medication.

4. The Internal Battlefield

Shame vs responsibility, crisis & safety protocols, suicide literacy.

5. Integration & Changing Systems

Meaning-making, advocacy, and building a new future.

60–75 minutes per session • Full facilitator scripts & participant materials provided
